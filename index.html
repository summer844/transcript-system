üìã Personal Information Collection and Use Agreement

YISS & WAY values the protection and privacy of the applicant's personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act. Please carefully read the information below.

1. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

Yonsei International Summer School and Winter Abroad at Yonsei ("YISS & WAY") will access and process your personal information to issue your academic transcript. This includes the necessary data collection for personal identification and academic administration purposes.

2. Items to be Collected

Name, Gender, Nationality, Contact number, E-mail address, Date of Birth, Home Institution, Mailing Address.

3. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

Collected personal information will be kept and used semi-permanently for the purposes of issuing transcripts, certificates, and academic affairs.

4. Right to Disagree and its Disadvantage

You have the right to disagree with the collection and use of personal information. However, if you disagree, you will be restricted from receiving a transcript from YISS and WAY.
